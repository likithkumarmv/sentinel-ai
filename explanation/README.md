### 🌟 Quick Executive Summary
**SentinelAI** is a premium **AI-Powered Virtual Boardroom Interview Simulator**. 

Instead of traditional, boring text-based quizzes, SentinelAI places you in a simulated 3D courtroom-like environment with three distinct AI Interviewers (The Architect, The Manager, and The Observer). Using your standard webcam, the system monitors your **posture**, **gaze**, **blink rates**, and **emotional composure** in real-time, creating a unified technical and psychological evaluation report to prepare you for high-stakes corporate recruitment under pressure.
