# 🎯 Aim of the Project

### 🏆 What is our ultimate goal?

The main aim of **SentinelAI** is to build a unified, automated **Job Interview Simulator** that objectively evaluates a candidate's technical skills and behavioral composure at the exact same time.

By replacing traditional, static preparation with a realistic 3D virtual boardroom panel, the project aims to:
*   Provide a **safe, bias-free sandbox** for candidates to practice speaking under pressure.
*   Offer recruiters a **scientific, highly detailed evaluation report** that maps out not just what a candidate said, but how confidently they behaved.
*   Combine advanced Artificial Intelligence (LLMs) and real-time Computer Vision (webcam posture and eye tracking) into one simple, cohesive, and premium web application.
