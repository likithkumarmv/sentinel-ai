# 📚 Literature Survey

SentinelAI is built upon established research in artificial intelligence, computer vision, and behavioral psychology. Here is a simplified summary of the concepts and technologies that inspire the platform:

### 1. Multi-Agent Systems (AI Collaboration)
*   **Concept:** Standard AI chat systems rely on a single agent conversation. However, research (like Stanford's generative agent architectures) proves that splitting tasks among distinct, specialized agents (e.g., a "Technical expert" and a "Behavioral analyst") yields much more accurate, structured, and high-fidelity evaluations.
*   **Application:** SentinelAI divides duties between **The Architect**, **The Manager**, and **The Observer** to simulate a real, multifaceted panel.

### 2. Micro-Expression & Emotion Analysis (Psychology)
*   **Concept:** The psychologist Paul Ekman discovered that humans express seven basic universal micro-expressions (Neutral, Happy, Sad, Angry, Surprised, Fearful, Disgusted) that leak our true emotional states, especially under pressure.
*   **Application:** SentinelAI uses real-time facial feature tracking to identify these expressions and calculate an "Emotion Composure Score" during difficult questions.

### 3. Biomechanics & Kinematics (Physical Telemetry)
*   **Concept:** Studies in ergonomics and occupational biomechanics show that stress correlates directly with micro-movements, high eye-blink frequency (normal is 15–20 blinks per minute; stress elevates this), and severe slouching or neck flexion.
*   **Application:** SentinelAI applies kinematic equations to webcam coordinate feeds to calculate active stress spikes, blink deviations, and spinal physical fatigue.

### 4. Speech Recognition & Natural Language Processing
*   **Concept:** OpenAI's Whisper model and modern speech analysis systems demonstrate that speech pacing (ideal is 130–150 words per minute) and the frequency of filler words are primary indicators of a speaker's structural clarity and confidence.
*   **Application:** The platform transcribes candidate answers and uses linguistic parsing to penalize rambling and prompt conversational clarity.
