# 🔍 Source of the Problem

### 📍 Where does this problem come from?

The core problems in modern hiring and interview preparation stem from three main sources:

1. **Human Subjectivity and Bias:**
   Interviews are conducted by human panels who are prone to exhaustion, mood swings, and subconscious biases. Decisions are frequently made based on "gut feelings" rather than objective, balanced metrics.

2. **The Shift to Remote Hiring (Webcams):**
   In online video interviews, it is much harder for recruiters to gauge soft skills, active listening, and physiological comfort. Candidates struggle with webcam positioning, keeping consistent eye contact, and showing natural confidence.

3. **Ineffective Preparation Tools:**
   Almost all preparation platforms (like LeetCode or HackerRank) only measure if your code runs. They do not prepare candidates for a panel of interviewers asking challenging follow-up questions, interrupting them when they ramble, or watching their physiological reactions.
