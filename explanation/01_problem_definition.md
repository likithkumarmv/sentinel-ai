# 💡 Problem Definition

### ❓ What is the main challenge?
When people prepare for job interviews, they face two massive hurdles:
1. **Technical stress:** Answering difficult coding or engineering questions in real-time.
2. **Behavioral anxiety:** Staying calm, maintaining good body language, avoiding filler words (like *"um"*, *"like"*), and keeping eye contact.

Traditional interview preparation is broken:
*   **Static quizzes** can't simulate the psychological pressure of a real panel interview.
*   **Human mock interviews** are expensive, hard to schedule, and highly subjective (suffer from personal bias).
*   **Standard online test platforms** completely ignore body language, eye contact, and emotional stability, which are critical for determining a candidate's fit in a high-stakes corporate environment.

### 📉 The Result
Candidates who are technically brilliant often fail because they freeze under pressure, while others who practice rote memorization pass tests but fail to communicate effectively under real stress.
